# Board Cybersecurity Oversight Framework

## 1. Board Responsibilities
The Board is responsible for:
• Oversight of cybersecurity risk
• Approval of risk appetite
• Review of major incidents
• Ensuring adequate resources

## 2. Reporting Frequency
• Quarterly cybersecurity updates
• Immediate notification of critical incidents

## 3. Information Presented to the Board
• Top enterprise cyber risks
• Regulatory compliance status
• Incident trends
• Third-party risk exposure
• Program maturity
