# Cybersecurity Governance Charter
Organization: FinTrust Capital
Industry: Financial Services

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Cybersecurity Governance Charter is to establish
a formal governance structure to oversee cybersecurity risk,
compliance obligations, and security strategy in alignment with
business objectives and regulatory requirements.

## 2. Scope
This charter applies to:
• All information systems
• Customer and financial data
• Employees and contractors
• Third-party service providers
• Cloud and on-premises infrastructure

## 3. Governance Objectives
• Protect confidentiality, integrity, and availability of financial data
• Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements (FFIEC, PCI DSS)
• Enable informed risk-based decision-making
• Support business growth securely

## 4. Governance Structure
Cybersecurity governance is overseen by:
• Board of Directors
• Executive Management
• Cybersecurity & Risk Committees
• Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)

## 5. Alignment with Business Strategy
Cybersecurity is treated as a business risk and an enabler of trust,
supporting:
• Digital banking services
• Payment processing
• Customer confidence
• Regulatory compliance

## 6. Regulatory and Framework Alignment
This governance program aligns with:
• NIST Cybersecurity Framework
• NIST Risk Management Framework
• ISO/IEC 27001
• FFIEC Cybersecurity Assessment Tool
• PCI DSS

## 7. Review and Maintenance
This charter shall be reviewed annually or upon significant
changes in:
• Regulatory requirements
• Business operations
• Threat landscape
