# Cybersecurity Risk Appetite Statement

FinTrust Capital has a LOW tolerance for risks that:
• Impact customer financial data
• Violate regulatory obligations
• Affect payment processing systems

FinTrust Capital has a MODERATE tolerance for risks related to:
• Innovation and digital transformation
• Adoption of cloud technologies
• Third-party service providers (with controls)

FinTrust Capital has NO tolerance for:
• Unauthorized disclosure of PII
• Intentional policy violations
• Regulatory non-compliance
