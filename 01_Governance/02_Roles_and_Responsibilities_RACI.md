# Cybersecurity Roles and Responsibilities (RACI)

## Key Roles

### Board of Directors
• Approves cybersecurity strategy and risk appetite
• Provides oversight of cyber risk
• Reviews major incidents

### CEO
• Accountable for enterprise risk
• Ensures cybersecurity aligns with business objectives

### CISO
• Owns cybersecurity program
• Reports cyber risk posture
• Leads incident response

### CIO
• Ensures secure IT operations
• Supports control implementation

### Risk Committee
• Reviews and approves risk acceptance
• Oversees enterprise risk management

### Internal Audit
• Provides independent assurance
• Evaluates control effectiveness

---

## RACI Matrix

| Activity | Board | CEO | CISO | CIO | Risk Committee | Audit |
|--------|------|-----|------|-----|---------------|-------|
| Cyber Strategy | A | R | C | C | C | I |
| Risk Acceptance | A | R | C | I | R | I |
| Incident Response | I | A | R | C | I | I |
| Compliance | I | A | R | C | C | R |
| Audit | I | I | C | C | I | R |

R = Responsible  
A = Accountable  
C = Consulted  
I = Informed
