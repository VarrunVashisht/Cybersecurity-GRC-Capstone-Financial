# Incident Response Tabletop Exercise

## Scenario Overview
A third-party payment processor notifies FinTrust Capital  
of a potential data breach impacting transaction data.

## Exercise Objectives
- Validate incident response procedures
- Test decision-making and escalation
- Assess communication effectiveness

## Key Discussion Points
- How is the incident classified?
- When is executive management notified?
- Are regulators notified?
- What do customers get told?

## Observations
- Escalation procedures were followed
- Communication gaps identified
- Vendor coordination needs improvement

## Outcome
Incident response plan updates recommended.
