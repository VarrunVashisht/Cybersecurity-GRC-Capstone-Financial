# Incident Response RACI Matrix

| Activity | CISO | Security Ops | IT Ops | Legal | Compliance | Communications |
|----------|------|---------------|--------|-------|------------|----------------|
| Incident Identification | A | R | I | I | I | I |
| Incident Analysis | A | R | C | I | I | I |
| Containment | A | R | R | I | I | I |
| Regulatory Notification | C | I | I | R | R | I |
| Customer Communication | I | I | I | C | C | R |
| Post-Incident Review | A | R | C | C | C | I |

**R** = Responsible  
**A** = Accountable  
**C** = Consulted  
**I** = Informed
