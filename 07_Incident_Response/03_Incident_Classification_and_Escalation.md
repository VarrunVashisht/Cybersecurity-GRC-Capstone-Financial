# Incident Classification and Escalation

## Severity Levels

### Low
- No customer data involved
- Minimal operational impact  
- **Example:** blocked malware

### Medium
- Limited system impact
- No confirmed data loss  
- **Example:** phishing with no compromise

### High
- Customer or financial data at risk
- Service disruption  
- **Example:** ransomware attempt

### Critical
- Confirmed data breach
- Regulatory notification required
- Significant financial or reputational impact

## Escalation Requirements
- High incidents → Executive Management
- Critical incidents → Board of Directors
