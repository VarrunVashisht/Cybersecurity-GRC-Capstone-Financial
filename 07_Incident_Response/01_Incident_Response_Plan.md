# Incident Response Plan

**Organization:** FinTrust Capital  
**Industry:** Financial Services  

## 1. Purpose
To establish a structured approach for identifying, responding to, containing, and recovering from cybersecurity incidents in order to minimize business, financial, and regulatory impact.

## 2. Scope
This plan applies to:
- All information systems
- Customer and financial data
- Employees, contractors, and third parties

## 3. Incident Response Objectives
- Rapid detection and containment
- Protection of customer data
- Compliance with regulatory obligations
- Clear communication and accountability

## 4. Incident Response Phases
- Preparation
- Detection and Analysis
- Containment
- Eradication
- Recovery
- Post-Incident Review

## 5. Incident Response Team (IRT)
- CISO – Incident Commander
- Security Operations – Technical response
- IT Operations – System recovery
- Legal & Compliance – Regulatory and legal guidance
- Communications – Internal and external messaging

## 6. Testing and Maintenance
The Incident Response Plan shall be tested at least annually  
through tabletop exercises and updated as required.
