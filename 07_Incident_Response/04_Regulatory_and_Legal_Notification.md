# Regulatory and Legal Notification Requirements

## 1. Notification Principles
- Timely
- Accurate
- Coordinated through Legal and Compliance

## 2. Notification Triggers
- Confirmed compromise of customer data
- Material impact to financial services
- Legal or regulatory obligation

## 3. Stakeholders to Notify
- Financial regulators (as applicable)
- Law enforcement (if required)
- Affected customers
- Cyber insurance provider

## 4. Approval Process
All external notifications must be reviewed and approved  
by Legal and Compliance prior to release.

## 5. Documentation
All notifications must be documented and retained  
for audit and regulatory review.
