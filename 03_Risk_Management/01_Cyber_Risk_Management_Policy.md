# Cyber Risk Management Policy
Organization: FinTrust Capital
Industry: Financial Services

## 1. Purpose
This policy defines the approach for identifying, assessing,
managing, and monitoring cybersecurity risks to protect
FinTrust Capital’s information assets and comply with
regulatory requirements.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to:
• All information systems
• Financial and customer data
• Employees and contractors
• Third-party service providers

## 3. Risk Management Objectives
• Identify cybersecurity risks proactively
• Reduce risks to acceptable levels
• Enable informed risk-based decision-making
• Support regulatory compliance

## 4. Risk Ownership
• Business units own risks
• CISO facilitates risk management
• Risk Committee approves risk treatment
• Board provides oversight

## 5. Risk Appetite Alignment
Cyber risks shall be managed in accordance with the
organization’s approved Cybersecurity Risk Appetite Statement.
