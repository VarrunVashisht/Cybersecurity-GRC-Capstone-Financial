# Risk Acceptance and Escalation Process

## 1. Risk Acceptance Criteria
Risks may be accepted if:
- Residual risk is within risk appetite
- Mitigation cost outweighs benefit
- Temporary compensating controls exist

## 2. Risk Acceptance Authority
- Low risk – Business Owner
- Medium risk – CISO
- High risk – Risk Committee
- Critical risk – Board of Directors

## 3. Risk Acceptance Documentation
All accepted risks must include:
- Risk description
- Justification
- Acceptance duration
- Approval authority

## 4. Escalation Triggers
Risks must be escalated if:
- Residual risk exceeds appetite
- Regulatory impact exists
- Customer data is affected
