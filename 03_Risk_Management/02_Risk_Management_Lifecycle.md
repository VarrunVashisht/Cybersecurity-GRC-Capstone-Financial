# Cyber Risk Management Lifecycle

## 1. Risk Identification
• Identify assets, threats, and vulnerabilities
• Include internal and external threats
• Consider third-party and cloud risks

## 2. Risk Analysis
• Assess likelihood of occurrence
• Assess business impact (financial, regulatory, reputational)
• Determine inherent risk level

## 3. Risk Evaluation
• Compare risk against risk appetite
• Prioritize risks for treatment

## 4. Risk Treatment
Risks may be:
• Mitigated
• Transferred
• Accepted
• Avoided

## 5. Risk Monitoring
• Continuous monitoring of key risks
• Regular reassessments
• Metrics and reporting

## 6. Risk Reporting
• Regular reporting to management
• Escalation of high risks to Risk Committee and Board
