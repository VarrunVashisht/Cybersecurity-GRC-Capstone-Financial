# Third-Party Cyber Risk Management

## 1. Purpose
To identify, assess, and manage cybersecurity risks introduced
by third-party service providers.

## 2. Vendor Risk Tiering
- **High Risk:** Access to customer data, payment processing
- **Medium Risk:** Business support services
- **Low Risk:** No data or system access

## 3. Due Diligence Activities
- Security questionnaires
- Compliance reviews (PCI, ISO)
- Contractual security clauses

## 4. Ongoing Monitoring
- Annual reassessments
- Incident notifications
- Performance reviews

## 5. Regulatory Expectations
The program aligns with FFIEC guidance for third-party risk
management.
