# Regulatory Examination Preparedness

## 1. Objective
To ensure readiness for cybersecurity regulatory examinations with minimal disruption.

## 2. Key Focus Areas
- Governance and board oversight
- Risk management practices
- Control effectiveness
- Incident response readiness
- Third-party risk management

## 3. Documentation Readiness
- Policies and procedures
- Risk register
- Audit reports
- Incident logs
- Metrics and board reports

## 4. Examination Support
- Single point of contact
- Timely and accurate responses
- Transparent communication

## 5. Post-Examination Actions
- Review examiner feedback
- Address identified gaps
- Update program improvements
