# Risk-Based Cybersecurity Audit Plan

## 1. Audit Planning Approach
The audit plan is developed using:
- Enterprise cyber risk register
- Key Risk Indicators (KRIs)
- Prior audit findings
- Regulatory expectations

## 2. High-Risk Audit Areas
- Third-Party Risk Management
- Access Control and Identity Management
- Incident Response and Breach Management
- Cloud Security Controls

## 3. Medium-Risk Audit Areas
- Security awareness training
- Patch and vulnerability management
- Change management

## 4. Low-Risk Audit Areas
- Documentation reviews
- Awareness materials

## 5. Audit Frequency
- **High risk** – Annual
- **Medium risk** – Every 2 years
- **Low risk** – As needed
