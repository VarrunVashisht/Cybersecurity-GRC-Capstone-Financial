# Cybersecurity Control Testing Methodology

## 1. Control Design Testing
Evaluates whether a control is:
- Properly designed
- Aligned to risk
- Documented and approved

## 2. Control Operating Effectiveness
Evaluates whether the control:
- Is implemented
- Operates consistently
- Produces evidence

## 3. Testing Techniques
- Policy and procedure review
- Evidence inspection
- Interviews
- Sampling and re-performance

## 4. Sampling Approach
- Judgmental sampling for high-risk controls
- Random sampling for operational controls

## 5. Evidence Requirements
- Evidence must be complete, accurate, and timely
