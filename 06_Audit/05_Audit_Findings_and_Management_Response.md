# Audit Findings and Management Response

**Title:** Incomplete Third-Party Risk Assessments

### Description

### Risk
Increased exposure to third-party cyber incidents.

### Severity
High

## Management Response
Management agrees with the finding.

- Enhance tracking process
- Update third-party risk procedures

## Target Completion Date
90 days
