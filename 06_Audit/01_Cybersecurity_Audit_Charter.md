# Cybersecurity Audit Charter  
**Organization:** FinTrust Capital

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this charter is to define the authority, responsibility, and scope of cybersecurity audits to provide independent assurance over the effectiveness of cybersecurity governance, risk management, and controls.

## 2. Authority
The Internal Audit function is authorized by the Board Audit Committee to:
- Access all systems, records, and personnel
- Conduct cybersecurity audits independently
- Report findings without management interference

## 3. Independence
Internal Audit operates independently from operational management and reports functionally to the Board Audit Committee.

## 4. Scope
Cybersecurity audits may include:
- Governance and risk management
- Security controls and policies
- Incident response and resilience
- Third-party risk management
- Regulatory compliance

## 5. Standards
Audits are conducted in accordance with:
- Institute of Internal Auditors (IIA) Standards
- NIST guidance
- Financial regulatory expectations

## 6. Reporting
Audit results are reported to:
- Executive management
- Audit Committee
- Board of Directors
