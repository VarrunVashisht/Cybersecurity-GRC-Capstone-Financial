# NIST CSF – Target State Profile

## IDENTIFY
• Complete asset inventory
• Formal risk assessments annually
• Integrated enterprise risk management
Target Maturity: REPEATABLE

## PROTECT
• Strong IAM and least privilege
• Continuous security awareness
• Data classification and encryption
Target Maturity: REPEATABLE

## DETECT
• Centralized logging (SIEM)
• Continuous monitoring
Target Maturity: REPEATABLE

## RESPOND
• Tested incident response plans
• Defined escalation procedures
Target Maturity: REPEATABLE

## RECOVER
• Tested disaster recovery plans
• Business continuity integration
Target Maturity: REPEATABLE

## Target Objective
Achieve a cybersecurity posture aligned with regulatory
expectations for financial institutions.
