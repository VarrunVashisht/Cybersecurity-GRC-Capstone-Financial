# Cybersecurity Gap Analysis and Roadmap

## Key Gaps Identified
• Incomplete asset inventory
• Inconsistent risk assessments
• Limited detection capabilities
• Untested incident response plans

## Risk Impact
• Increased regulatory risk
• Delayed incident detection
• Higher operational disruption

## 3-Year Roadmap

### Year 1 (Foundation)
• Formalize risk management process
• Improve governance documentation
• Establish centralized logging
• Conduct incident response tabletop

### Year 2 (Enhancement)
• Automate controls monitoring
• Enhance third-party risk management
• Expand security awareness training

### Year 3 (Optimization)
• Advanced threat detection
• Continuous control validation
• Mature metrics and reporting
