# Cybersecurity Program Charter
Organization: FinTrust Capital
Industry: Financial Services

## 1. Program Purpose
The Cybersecurity Program is established to manage cyber risks,
protect financial and customer data, ensure regulatory compliance,
and support secure business operations.

## 2. Program Objectives
• Reduce cybersecurity risk to acceptable levels
• Maintain compliance with financial regulations
• Protect customer trust and brand reputation
• Enable secure digital innovation

## 3. Program Scope
The program covers:
• Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC)
• Security architecture and controls
• Incident response and resilience
• Third-party risk management
• Security awareness and training

## 4. Framework Alignment
The program is aligned with:
• NIST Cybersecurity Framework (Primary)
• NIST Risk Management Framework
• ISO/IEC 27001
• FFIEC guidance
• PCI DSS

## 5. Roles and Ownership
• Board of Directors – Oversight
• Executive Management – Accountability
• CISO – Program ownership
• Business Units – Risk ownership

## 6. Success Criteria
• Reduction in high-risk findings
• Improved audit outcomes
• Faster incident response times
• Increased security maturity

## 7. Review Cycle
The program is reviewed annually or upon major business or
regulatory changes.
