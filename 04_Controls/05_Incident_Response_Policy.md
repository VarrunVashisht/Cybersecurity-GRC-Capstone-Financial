# Incident Response Policy

## 1. Purpose
To ensure timely and effective response to cybersecurity  
incidents.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all security incidents impacting systems,  
data, or services.

## 3. Incident Categories
- Data breach
- Malware
- Phishing
- System compromise

## 4. Roles
- **CISO** – Incident owner
- **Incident Response Team** – Execution
- **Legal & Compliance** – Regulatory notification

## 5. Reporting
All incidents must be reported immediately.

## 6. Testing
Incident response procedures shall be tested annually.
