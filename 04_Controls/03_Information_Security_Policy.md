# Information Security Policy  
**Organization:** FinTrust Capital

## 1. Purpose
To establish management direction and support for information  
security in alignment with business objectives and regulatory  
requirements.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to:
- All employees and contractors
- All information assets
- All systems and networks

## 3. Policy Statements
- Information must be protected against unauthorized access
- Security controls shall be implemented based on risk
- Regulatory and contractual obligations must be met
- Security incidents must be reported promptly

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities
- **Board** – Oversight
- **Executive Management** – Accountability
- **CISO** – Policy enforcement
- **Employees** – Compliance

## 5. Compliance
Violations may result in disciplinary action.

## 6. Review
Reviewed annually or upon significant change.
