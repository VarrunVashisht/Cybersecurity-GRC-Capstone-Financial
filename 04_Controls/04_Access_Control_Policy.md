# Access Control Policy

## 1. Purpose
To ensure access to information systems is authorized,
appropriate, and based on business need.

## 2. Principles
- Least privilege
- Role-based access
- Segregation of duties

## 3. User Access Management
- Access requires management approval
- Access reviewed periodically
- Access removed upon termination

## 4. Authentication
- Multi-factor authentication required
- Strong password standards enforced

## 5. Monitoring
- Access activities logged
- Unauthorized access investigated
