# Control Effectiveness Assessment

## 1. Assessment Criteria
Controls are evaluated as:
- Effective
- Partially Effective
- Ineffective

## 2. Assessment Methods
- Policy review
- Evidence inspection
- Interviews
- Sampling

## 3. Sample Assessment

**Control:** Multi-Factor Authentication  
**Result:** Effective  

**Evidence:**
- MFA enforced on critical systems
- Logs reviewed
- Exceptions documented

## 4. Reporting
Control weaknesses are reported to management  
with remediation plans.
