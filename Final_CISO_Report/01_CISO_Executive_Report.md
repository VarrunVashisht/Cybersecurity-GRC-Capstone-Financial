## Executive Summary
FinTrust Capital has implemented an enterprise-wide Cybersecurity  
Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) program aligned with  
financial regulatory expectations and industry best practices.

The program establishes strong governance, risk-based decision  
making, effective control implementation, and executive-level  
oversight to protect customer data, financial systems, and  
organizational reputation.

Overall cybersecurity risk is currently assessed as **MODERATE** and  
within the Board-approved risk appetite.

## Business Context
FinTrust Capital operates in a highly regulated financial  
environment with increasing reliance on digital platforms,  
cloud services, and third-party vendors. Cybersecurity is treated  
as a critical business risk and strategic enabler.

## CISO Assurance Statement
Based on current assessments, management confirms that  
cybersecurity risks are being identified, managed, and monitored  
in alignment with regulatory and business expectations.
