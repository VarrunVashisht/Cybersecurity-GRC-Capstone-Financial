# Key CISO Decisions and Recommendations

## Executive Decisions Required
- Approve continued investment in third-party risk management
- Endorse enhanced security awareness training
- Support expansion of monitoring and detection capabilities

## Key Recommendations
- Strengthen vendor monitoring automation
- Increase frequency of incident response exercises
- Advance cybersecurity metrics maturity

## Risk Acceptance
All accepted risks have documented approvals and defined review  
periods in line with governance requirements.
