# Key Risk Indicators (KRIs)

## 1. Number of High-Risk Open Findings
- **Description:** Unresolved high-risk audit or assessment findings
- **Threshold:** ≤ 3
- **Risk Addressed:** Regulatory and operational risk

## 2. Critical Vulnerabilities Outstanding
- **Description:** Critical vulnerabilities older than SLA
- **Threshold:** ≤ 5
- **Risk Addressed:** Exploitation risk

## 3. High-Risk Third-Party Vendors
- **Description:** Vendors rated high risk without remediation
- **Threshold:** ≤ 2
- **Risk Addressed:** Supply chain risk

## 4. Phishing Click Rate
- **Description:** Percentage of users clicking phishing simulations
- **Threshold:** ≤ 5%
- **Risk Addressed:** Credential compromise

## 5. Incident Trend Increase
- **Description:** Quarter-over-quarter increase in incidents
- **Threshold:** No sustained upward trend
- **Risk Addressed:** Control degradation
