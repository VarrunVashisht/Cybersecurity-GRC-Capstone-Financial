# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

## 1. Patch Management Compliance
- **Description:** Percentage of systems patched within SLA
- **Target:** ≥ 95%
- **Business Value:** Reduces vulnerability exploitation risk

## 2. Incident Response Time
- **Description:** Average time to contain security incidents
- **Target:** < 4 hours for high-severity incidents
- **Business Value:** Limits financial and reputational damage

## 3. Security Awareness Training Completion
- **Description:** Percentage of employees completing training
- **Target:** 100%
- **Business Value:** Reduces phishing and human error risk

## 4. Audit Findings Closure Rate
- **Description:** Percentage of audit issues closed on time
- **Target:** ≥ 90%
- **Business Value:** Demonstrates control effectiveness

## 5. Third-Party Risk Reviews Completed
- **Description:** Percentage of high-risk vendors reviewed annually
- **Target:** 100%
- **Business Value:** Reduces vendor-related exposure
