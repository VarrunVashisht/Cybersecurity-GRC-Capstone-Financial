# Cybersecurity Metrics Strategy  
**Organization:** FinTrust Capital

## 1. Purpose
To define a structured approach for measuring cybersecurity  
performance and risk in a way that supports business and  
regulatory decision-making.

## 2. Objectives
- Provide visibility into cybersecurity posture
- Support risk-based decisions
- Enable executive and board oversight
- Meet regulatory reporting expectations

## 3. Metrics Categories
- **Operational Metrics** – day-to-day security performance
- **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)** – program effectiveness
- **Key Risk Indicators (KRIs)** – emerging and residual risks

## 4. Target Audience
- **Operational teams** – detailed metrics
- **Executive management** – summarized metrics
- **Board of Directors** – risk-focused metrics

## 5. Guiding Principles
- Metrics must be actionable
- Metrics must be accurate and repeatable
- Metrics must align with business risk
